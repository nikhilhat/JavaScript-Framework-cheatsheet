The Indo-German Chamber of Commerce (IGCC) invites you to the Virtual Kick-off Event “Supporting India’s Agriculture Sector against Post-Harvest Losses.” The event launches an initiative organised by the IGCC that seeks to support farmers in India by using innovative technology to prevent post-harvest losses along selected agricultural value chains.  The virtual kick-off event “Supporting India’s Agriculture Sector against Post-Harvest Losses” is organised within the framework of the “Innovation platform and hybrid trade fair - Reducing post-harvest losses in India” Project assisted by the German Government, carried out by the Indo-German Chamber of Commerce on behalf of the Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) GmbH.
